Le Havre

Background

Named after a French Port where many French Luxury cruise liners were launched in the 1930’s, Le Havre is a sans serif that is geometric and inspired by the golden era of the passenger ship. Designed by Jeremy Dooley in 2009, it features an art deco flair that is a unique look into the past for inspirations put into a new contemporary design.
